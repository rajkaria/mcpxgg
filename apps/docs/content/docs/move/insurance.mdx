title: "mcpx::insurance"
description: Insurance pool — outage and failed-call payouts, funded by 0.5% of every call.
---

`contracts/sources/insurance.move`

`InsurancePool<T>` receives the insurance share (default 0.5%,
configurable). Two caps: `AdminCap` (top-up / emergency drain) and
`InsurancePayerCap` (minted by the admin to oracle addresses for routine
payouts without admin sign-off).

## Key entry functions

- `initialize<T>(&AdminCap, ctx)` — create a per-coin pool.
- `mint_payer_cap(&AdminCap, recipient, ctx)` — grant payout authority.
- `top_up<T>(pool, contribution)` — anyone can donate.
- `pay_out<T>(...)` — routine outage payout (`InsurancePayerCap`-gated).

Failed-call refunds flow from `settlement::claim_for_failed_call`, proven
by a soulbound receipt and pre-capped to the pool balance.

See the [Insurance concept](/docs/concepts/insurance).

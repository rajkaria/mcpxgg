---
title: Insurance pool
description: 0.5% of every settled call funds outage and failed-call payouts.
---

The **InsurancePool** receives the insurance share — by default 0.5% of
every settled call (`insurance_bps`, configurable in `PlatformConfig`).
It pays out for verified outages, failed-call refunds, and slashed-stake
recipients.

Move module: `mcpx::insurance` (`contracts/sources/insurance.move`).

## Capabilities

- **`AdminCap`** (from the `admin` module) — emergency top-up / drain.
- **`InsurancePayerCap`** — minted by the admin to one or more oracle
  addresses; lets them authorize routine outage payouts without admin
  sign-off.

## Key entry functions

- `initialize<T>(&AdminCap, ctx)` — create a per-coin pool.
- `mint_payer_cap(&AdminCap, recipient, ctx)` — grant payout authority to
  an oracle.
- `top_up<T>(pool, contribution)` — anyone can donate to the pool.
- `pay_out<T>(...)` — routine outage payout, gated by `InsurancePayerCap`.

Failed-call refunds flow from `settlement::claim_for_failed_call`, which
proves the claim with a soulbound `CallReceipt` (`success == false`,
`refunded == false`) before the pool pays. The amount is pre-capped to
the pool balance.
